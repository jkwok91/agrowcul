What is agrowculture?

We grow hyper-local food networks.

to help communities get fresh vegetables delivered to their doorstep.

Why Local Food?

Locally grown food is better for everyone.

Eating food grown near you means less energy is used to transport, package and distribute it.

It also means fresher produce and increased food security, all while strengthening local commerce.

Support your community when you can and eat locally.
